# Atlas product voice

Atlas copy is calm, specific, and accountable. It avoids blame, hype, alarmism, and vague action labels.

## Do

- Say what happened and what the user can do next.
- Use action labels like `Suspend account`, `Review invoices`, or `Create risk note`.
- Keep destructive confirmations short and factual.

## Do not

- Do not write "You failed", "Oops", or "This is amazing!"
- Do not label critical controls as "Submit", "Process", or "Continue" when a specific verb is available.
- Do not hedge destructive consequences with "maybe" or "probably".
